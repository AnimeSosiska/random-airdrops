description=
description=[h2]What this mod can do:[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] You can change perfomance tickrate
description=[*] After how much hours to remove the airdrops
description=[*] Chance to spawn every hour
description=[*] Spawn coordinates of every airdrop (Lua Folder)
description=[*] You can change the loot tables (Lua Folder)
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Features:[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*] Airdrops never spawn above each other
description=[*] Airdrops doesnt despawn if player is close
description=[*] Message with direction to airdrop above player when airdrop plane is too far away
description=[*] Random radio frequency that tells if there's an airdrop going to be dropped (You can find it in random radios and walkie talkies)
description=[*] Airplane sound when the airdrop spawn
description=[*] If the servers close, old airdrops will be deleted automatically (configurable)
description=[*] Fully customizable loot table (see the github)
description=[*] Fully customizable spawn coordinates (see the github)
description=[*] Or you can use standart loot table and spawn coordinates (this option is enabled on default in sandbox settings)
description=[/list]
